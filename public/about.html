About us

Snowdrifters is a non profit club that supports snowmobiling around Cedar Rapids and Iowa City that started in the mid 1970s. Recent awared of ISSA Club of the Year 17/18 & ASCA Club of the Year 2018 with a largemembership.

Club meetings every Thursday of each month @ 7:30 PM. See Club Meeting page for meeting location.
